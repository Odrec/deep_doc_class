5th step: Answering Question

Question: How could you calculate backwards to obtain the light spectrum of the visual stimulus that
induced those activations?

Answer: Perform the last computation backwards, that means computing the derivation of the cone acti-
vations and then dividing by the estimated values of the absorption spectra of the 3 cone types.

Difficulties: One difficulty could be that the estimated values for the cone absorption spectra for the 3 cone
types are different and therefore you can get values for the light spectrum of the visual stimulus that differ
from the orginial ones. If you change the order of the cones you will as well get other results
