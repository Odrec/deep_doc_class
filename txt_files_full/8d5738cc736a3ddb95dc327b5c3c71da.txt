Contrast 

1.

Fourier optics and Fourier filtering is the principle upon which many 

microscopy techniques are based: Filtering certain spatial 

frequencies enhances the contrast for structures of the chosen size 

 

2.

 Examples are darkfield illumination and phase contrast microscopy, 

polarization microscopy (employing birefringence) and more 

advanced/complex schemes like differential intereference contrast . 

 

3. Color can be used in addition (like in Rheinberg illumination).
