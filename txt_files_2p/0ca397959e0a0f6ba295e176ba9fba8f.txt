Das erste  Bild von Sprache

What is a language?  A social phenomenon 
which is part of the natural  history  of human 
beings; a sphere of human  action, wherein 
people utter  strings of vocal sounds, or 
inscribe  strings of marks,  and wherein  people 
respond by thought or action to the sounds 
or marks  which they observe  to have  been  so 
produced.  (Lewis  1975, 164)
