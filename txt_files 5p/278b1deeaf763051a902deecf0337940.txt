SPOT 

(SPOT IMAGE) 

w  Area covered by one scene 60 * 60 
w  Temporal resolution:  

- vertical look angle: 26 days 
- oblique look angle: 4 days  

w  Orbit nearly polar and sun-synchronic (823 km 

high) 

w  Average spectral resolution: 

- 3 bands in XS mode  (0.50 - 0.89 um) 
- 1 band in panchromatic mode (0.50 - 0.75 um) 

w  Average spatial  resolution: 

- XS mode : 20 m 
- Panchromatic mode: 10 m 

w  Stereo capabilities 

(http://www.spotimage.fr)

Multi-spectral scanners 

Advantages of multi-spectral scanner data: 

w high spectral resolution 
w moderate temporal resolution 
w good processing software on the market 
w different sensors spectrally good compatible 

Disadvantages of multi-spectral scanner data: 

w recent data relative expensive 
w long ordering time 
w cloud coverage
