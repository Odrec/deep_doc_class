Defects in COL1A1 are a cause of Ehlers-Danlos syndrome type 1 (EDS1); also known as

Defects in COL1A1 are a cause of osteogenesis imperfecta type 1 (OI1).  
 
A dominantly inherited connective tissue disorder characterized by bone fragility and blue 
sclerae.  
 
Osteogenesis imperfecta type 1 is non-deforming with normal height or mild short stature, 
and no dentinogenesis imperfecta.
