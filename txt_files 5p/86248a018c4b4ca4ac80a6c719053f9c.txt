Las damas
Doña Ángela

• la dama duende

• protagonista femenina

• activa  motor de la acción

• viuda

• joven

• bella

• curiosa

•objetivos: 

„Calderón‘s phantom lady flits across the stage like
a bundle of vailed motion, a volatile packet of erotic
energy, chameleon-like in her duende-like nature. 
She is indeed like no other female character he was 
to imagine, and her femininity is central to the
themes and theatrical techniques of illusion in this
play.“ (Cascardi 1984: 25)
